SKYFLEET ELD provides a complete solution for managing your fleet in one place, including driver safety, GPS tracking, and ELD compliance. It features an intuitive device and application designed for ease of use.

With its wide range of useful features, SKYFLEET ELD helps you reduce risks, ensure legal compliance, cut down on fuel waste, streamline transportation operations, monitor vehicle health, enhance safety and security, and lower additional expenses.

SKYFLEET ELD features a robust device and a user-friendly application packed with valuable functions.

The SKYFLEET ELD device connects to the vehicle and gathers data directly from the commercial motor vehicle (CMV) engine. This data includes engine status (on/off), speed, operating hours, mileage, and other relevant details. The information is then transmitted to the SKYFLEET ELD application, where it is displayed and allows drivers or fleet managers to add supplementary details as needed.
